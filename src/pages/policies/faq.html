QNT42 Frequently Asked Questions

How can I sign up to the platform?

Just go to the registration page and fill out the signup form. It only takes a few moments.

After signing up, what should I do?

Verify your account by clicking on the link sent to your email and using the provided OTP Token. Once verified, log in and start creating and managing your notes.

How can I reset my password if I forget it?

For the current version, reset password feature is still in development, if you forget your password you can contact our support team. However for the right time we recommend you to keep your password safe and secure.

Does QNT42 collect or share my data with third parties?

No, QNT42 does not collect, sell, or share your data with third parties. All your notes are encrypted on our end before being stored.

How can I trust this platform with my data?

Your data is encrypted end-to-end, and only you have access to it. No one else, including us, can read your stored data.

How can I back up and restore my data?

You can export your encrypted data and store it securely on your side. When needed, you can import it back into the platform.

Can I use QNT42 offline?

No, the platform currently does not support offline usage. However, we are exploring options to implement this feature in future releases.

Is my data synchronized across multiple devices?

No, currently the platform does not support synchronization across multiple devices. Each device operates independently. However, we're still exploring options to implement this feature in future releases.

What platforms does QNT42 support?

QNT42 is currently available as a web application accessible through modern browsers.

Is there a mobile app for QNT42?

Currently, QNT42 is only available as a web application. However, we are considering developing mobile apps in the future based on user demand.

How secure is my data with QNT42?

Your data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption algorithms before being stored. We follow best security practices to ensure your information remains private and secure.

Can I share my notes with others?

Currently, note sharing is not supported. However, we are working on a secure sharing feature that will allow you to share selected notes with others while maintaining encryption.
