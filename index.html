    About QNT42

    QNT42 (Quick Notes Taking) is a lightweight, secure, and efficient note-taking web application designed for fast and easy note management.

    With a clean and intuitive interface, it lets you write, store, and manage notes securely with complete privacy, your data is encrypted and accessible only to you.

    Get Started

    Key Features

    Secure Authentication

    Register and login safely with email authentication. Your account, your control.

    Client-Side Encryption

    Encryption is applied directly in your side before any data is stored, ensuring complete privacy.

    Advanced Note Management

    Create, edit, and organize effortlessly your notes with titles, content, and keyword tags.

    Customizable Theming

    Multiple color themes to personalize your experience.

    Toggle between light and dark themes for comfortable viewing day or night.

    Responsive Design

    QNT42 is fully fluid, and adaptive design engineered for seamless performance across desktop, tablet, and mobile platforms.

    Data portability

    Comprehensive backup system with timestamped JSON exports and streamlined import capabilities.

    Cloud Data Storage & Sync

    Store data in a secure cloud database, allowing safe syncing across all your devices.
